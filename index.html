UWE Islamic Society

Supporting Muslim students at the University of the West of England by providing a welcoming community, Islamic education, and opportunities to grow spiritually and socially on campus.

Who We Are

UWE Islamic Society is a student-led organisation dedicated to creating an Islamically grounded environment for Muslim students and staff. The society aims to help students live a God-centred life while studying at university, while also sharing the beauty and values of Islam with the wider community. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

What We Do

Islamic Education

Weekly classes including Qur'an circles, Seerah sessions and workshops that help students strengthen their understanding of Islam.

Community Events

Talks, socials, Discover Islam Week, Islamophobia Awareness Month, and collaborative events with other Muslim societies in Bristol.

Ramadan Activities

Community iftars, Taraweeh prayers and spiritual programmes to support students during the blessed month of Ramadan.

Student Support

Helping Muslim students feel at home on campus by providing community, guidance and a safe space to practice their faith.

Our Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a campus where Muslim students are confident in their Islamic identity, practice their faith unapologetically, and feel supported spiritually, mentally and academically throughout their university journey. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
